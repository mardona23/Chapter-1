Welcome to Brawl Stars.

Brawl Stars is a popular mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Supercell, the creators of games like Clash of Clans, Clash Royale. Released globally in December 2018, it quickly gained a large following due to its fast-paced gameplay, easy-to-learn mechanics, and regular updates.
