General Facts

Ancient Companions: Dogs were domesticated around 20,000-40,000 years ago and are considered the first domesticated animal.Incredible Sense of Smell: Dogs' sense of smell is 10,000–100,000times more sensitive than humans'. Unique Nose Prints: A dog's nose print is as unique as a human fingerprint. Super Hearing: Dogs can hear frequencies between 40 Hz to 60 kHz, far beyond the range of human hearing. Color Vision: While not colorblind, dogs see fewer colors than humans—mostly shades of blue and yellow.
